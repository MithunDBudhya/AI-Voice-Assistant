AMAZON INDIA FAQ & SUPPORT PROCEDURES — OFFICIAL REFERENCE

Last Updated: May 2026

1. AMAZON PRIME MEMBERSHIP
Amazon Prime is a premium subscription program offering benefits to customers in India:
- Free delivery: Free One-Day, Two-Day, and Standard Delivery on eligible items. No minimum order value required.
- Prime Video & Prime Music: Unlimited ad-free music streaming and access to exclusive movies, TV shows, and Amazon Originals.
- Prime Reading: Free access to a curated selection of eBooks.
- Early Access: 24-hour early access to major sale events like Prime Day, Great Indian Festival, and Lightning Deals.
- Prime Pricing: Subscription costs ₹1,499 for 1 year, ₹599 for 3 months, or ₹299 for 1 month.

2. PAYMENT FAILURES AND DOUBLE CHARGES
If money is deducted from your bank account but the order is not placed (marked as "Payment Failed"):
- Do not panic. The money is held in a bank transit pool and is completely safe.
- UPI / Net Banking: Refund is initiated automatically and will revert to the original bank account within 3 to 5 business days.
- Credit / Debit Card: Reversal occurs automatically within 5 to 7 business days.
- Customers can check the refund status directly in the Amazon Pay dashboard under "Transactions". If the reversal is delayed, customer support can issue a Bank Reference Number (ARN/RRN) to trace the transaction with your bank.

3. TAX INVOICE AND WARRANTY CLAIMS
To claim warranty on any product purchased from Amazon India:
- Go to "Your Orders" on the Amazon app/website.
- Locate the specific product and click on "Download Invoice". Select "Tax Invoice" to download the PDF.
- Print the tax invoice and take it along with the product to any brand-authorized service center. The invoice serves as official proof of purchase and warranty start date.
- Note: Amazon India does not directly issue warranty cards. The invoice is sufficient for brand service centers across India.
